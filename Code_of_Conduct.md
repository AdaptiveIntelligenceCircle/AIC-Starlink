# Code of Conduct (AIC Community Standard)

## 1. Purpose

AIC-Starlink is a research and security engineering project focused on resilience, defense, and responsible innovation.

We expect all contributors and participants to treat each other with professionalism, respect, and discipline.

---

## 2. Expected Behavior

Participants are expected to:

- Use welcoming and inclusive language
- Respect different viewpoints and experiences
- Accept constructive criticism
- Focus on what is best for the project and its mission
- Show empathy toward other community members

---

## 3. Unacceptable Behavior

The following behaviors are unacceptable:

- Harassment, discrimination, or personal attacks
- Trolling, insulting comments, or deliberate disruption
- Publishing private information without permission
- Encouraging illegal activities or misuse of security research
- Any form of manipulation, intimidation, or abuse

---

## 4. Scope

This Code of Conduct applies to all spaces related to the project, including:

- GitHub issues and pull requests
- discussions and community channels
- documentation and communication spaces
- offline events related to AIC projects

---

## 5. Enforcement

Maintainers may remove, edit, or reject any contributions that violate this Code of Conduct.

Violations may result in:

- warnings
- temporary bans
- permanent bans
- removal from the organization

---

## 6. Reporting

To report violations, contact:

- **Security Team / Maintainers**
- Email: `security@aic.example` *(replace later)*

Reports will be handled confidentially.

---

## 7. Commitment

We are building security infrastructure.  
Discipline, responsibility, and respect are non-negotiable.
